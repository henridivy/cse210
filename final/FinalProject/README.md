The Language Assistant

Program Description: 
The Language Assistant is a program that helps users learn the basics of a chosen language. Users can choose from one of the five languages offered. Users will study and practice the terms given, taking quizzes along the way to help them master the material.

The Language Assistant offers the following 5 langauges: Spanish, French, Portuguese, Latin, and Filipino. The 5 levels of focus are: vocabulary, numbers and time, dates, short phrases, and self-introduction.

Program Function:
The game is split into 5 levels, each with a different focus. After studying in each level, the user will take a quiz and be awarded points for every correct answer. The user has to reach a milestone before moving on to the next level. Level 1 is the easiest, and offers the least amount of points. Level 5 is the hardest and offers the highest amount of points. After completing all the levels, the user will be giving a final exam comprising of everything they've learned. Badges are earned along the way after the completion of each level.
